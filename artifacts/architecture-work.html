Key Decisions & Tradeoffs

These are the decisions that demonstrate judgment.

Why time-bound share links?

Sharing is a major risk surface. Time + scope limits reduce blast radius and make access reviewable.

Scoped access Expiry Revocation

Why separate operational vs analytics?

Analytics needs broader access. Operational services must stay minimal, fast, and locked down.

Security boundary Performance Governance

Why IaC guardrails?

Humans drift. IaC and policy checks keep environments consistent and compliant.

Terraform Policy-as-code Repeatability
